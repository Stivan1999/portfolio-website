Wrong Place Wrong Time

I am halfway through this book at the time of writing this (10/8/2023). As I moved into a new apartment this fall, I picked this book while grocery shopping at Meijir. I instantly fell in love with it. It talks about a mother who witnesses her son committing a murder on an autumn day after midnight. After that, the mother starts waking up backward every morning and tries to find the clue to why her son would do what he did on that night. Thriller, Suspense, Mystery, and sudden plot twists are my perfect types in a story. If you try this book, do not forget to red it while eating warm cinnamon rolls and sipping on a cup of tea ðŸ˜‰.

The Midnight Library

A book about the multiverse theory? Yes please, this type of story is my cup of tea. Nora decides to end her life around midnight because her life is miserable (or at least she thinks so). She then finds herself at this place called the midnight library. Every book in this library is a different version of herself that made different choices in life. She gets to discover the alternate lives she could have led. Will she find the ultimate universe where she is happy and her life is not a ruin? This book really made think about my life and the choices I made, make, and keep making. What would have happened if I decided to go to a different school? If I have said no to the first job offer I got? decided not to leave my country, friends, and family to come to the US and pursue my goals? This book is an absolute eye-opener and will leave you thinking for days.

It is so hard to say anything about this book without spoiling. So I will leave it at this, if you love mystery like I do then you will absolutely love this book.
